help & controls
modes
regenerate
bottom-right button or r rolls a new world seed.
follow creature
tap any creature to track it indefinitely. Settings → follow pauses the sim while choosing. esc releases follow.
inspect specimen
shift + click any creature or flora to open its inspect view in a new tab.
first-person stroll
f enters/exits. wasd walks · shift moves faster · mouse looks · esc exits.
photo mode
p hides the ui and freezes animation. s saves a png while in photo mode. p or esc exits.
manual pause
space pauses or resumes the simulation outside photo/stroll/selection modes.
