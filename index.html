help & controls
modes
same biome
↻ rolls a fresh seed that stays in the current biome, ignoring biome-filter chips.
next biome
⇄ or r steps to the next enabled biome, looping back to the first after the last.
locator
l opens the entity locator panel. Click a type to fly the camera to the nearest instance. Creatures enter follow mode. tab cycles through all instances of the selected type. esc closes the panel.
follow creature
tap any creature to track it indefinitely. Settings → follow pauses the sim while choosing. esc releases follow.
inspect specimen
shift + click any creature or flora to open its inspect view in a new tab.
first-person stroll
f enters/exits. wasd walks · shift moves faster · mouse looks · esc exits.
photo mode
p hides the ui and freezes animation. Click or use the save button to capture a png. p or esc exits.
manual pause
space pauses or resumes the simulation outside photo/stroll/selection modes.
