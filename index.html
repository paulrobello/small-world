help & controls
modes
regenerate
bottom-right button rolls a new world seed.
click-to-focus
tap any creature for a few seconds of close camera focus.
follow
settings → follow → tap a creature to track it indefinitely.
first-person stroll
press f (or settings → first-person stroll). wasd to walk · mouse to look · f or esc to exit.
photo mode
press p to hide the ui and freeze every animation. press s while in photo mode to save a png to your downloads. p or esc to exit.
controls
drag
rotate the camera.
scroll · pinch
zoom in / out.

gently wakes drowsing creatures.
p
toggle photo mode.
s
save the current frame as a png (photo mode only).
f
enter or exit first-person stroll.
esc
cancel focus · exit any mode · close panels.
